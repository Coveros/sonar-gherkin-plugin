There should be one single Then step per scenario. If you feel compelled to add more, it is usually a sign that you should split the scenario up in multiple scenarios or that some Then steps could be converted into more complete source code methods.

Scenario: Add a nice bike to my cart
  Given I am a customer
  When I go to the nice bike product page
  Then I add the nice bike to my cart
  And I should see the nice bike in my cart  # Noncompliant

Compliant Solution

Scenario: Add a nice bike to my cart
  Given I am a customer
  When I am on the nice bike product page
  Then I can add the nice bike to my cart
